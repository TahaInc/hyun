About us



Hey there, let me introduce myself, my name is Jeremie Hamel, and I am the founder of HYUN, a company where there’s always a story behind a style. It’s based in Ottawa, Canada. Let’s start from the beginning because every step counts and means everything to me.


A couple months ago, the idea of creating a brand popped in my head, but I never really went and committed to that plan. I was just being a very lazy person, without knowing what true potential I had, and the life-experience to acquire during this process, of being able to create a brand from scratch. As a korean teenager, there isn’t much of a korean community around my area, or in my everyday life, so it was somewhat hard to really find a connection with your culture. I know for a fact that many of us have their own culture that they surely appreciate and embrace. But for me, it wasn’t the case, I wanted to be able to have or represent something that had some quite a link to who I truly was. This is the reason, I called this brand: HYUN; which is basically my first name in korean.


It was appealing to me, because I knew that I would be able to connect and really have a passion for every product I created. It was a motivation for me to be able to represent what I do, since I never really had the chance to stand out on my own. I knew this was a really beneficial project for me to do. I needed to start somewhere, because honestly, I was pretty lost in what I had to do because I've never done anything of this size. I had to research everything about ‘’How to make a brand’’ on youtube, on the web, I was basically a rookie at this. After hours and hours of different research made on the subject, I finally felt ready to make the first move. But it wasn’t after a couple weeks that I actually starting planning, if I’m being truly honest.


I started in January, 2019. I brought a friend along who was a genius at computer programming that was able to help me start a website. Then, I had to plan some designs, what I wanted to sell, and make a plan basically. After that, I joined facebook groups, instagram accounts, on the hunt for a supplier that could create, from scratch, clothing products that I wanted, and ended up finding one, after many days on the lookout. My goal, personally, wasn’t to just slap a logo on a pre-made t-shirt (which is totally fine) I wanted to offer more, so it could be a challenge for me. I started drawing designs on papers and got them drawn and edited online to be able to send it to my supplier. That took a couple of weeks, since I also needed to buy samples, for quality check, which costed a pretty penny. It was primordial to do this, since I did not just want to offer a cheap quality product, since, well, nobody likes that. It took a couple tries, and a lot of communication with my supplier almost everyday. The website was getting done at the same time, and I was able to get some live samples shipped to my door, and I was pretty proud. Once I was happy with everything, every single little detail on the products counted. I got a lot of feedback from my friends, which were highly considered.


Throughout this process, I had to bargain a lot to acquire a price that was really fair for the customers and myself. I wanted to bring a high-quality product, for a cheap and fair price. Since every single piece is completely made from a roll of material to its physical shape, it was quite hard to get a low price, but fortunately, I got what I wanted! Now that I am here finalizing the website and wondering if even anyone read this, this is my story, and I hope you were able to understand my perspective and reasoning behind this amazing brand. I appreciate all the love and support, and hopefully see one of you guys embrace what I created. It’s more than just a company, it’s a story.
